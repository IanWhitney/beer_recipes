# Malcontent 2013

## Recipe:
- 10kg Marris
- DME to adjust OG up
- 2 hopshots @ FWH
- 2 oz EKG @ FWH
- .24g whirlfloc @ 10 minutes
- 1.5g nutrient @ 10 minutes

## Mash:
- 60 min @ 152
- 2.5 l/kg
- 25 l strike water @ 165

## Sparge:
- No sparge

## Boil:
- 14.7 L of wort with a pre-boil gravity around 1.087
- 25% evap rate
- Post-boil 11 L of wort at 1.160 OG

### Strike Water
